About Me

I'm Catherine Olsson: a coder, a scientist, an extrovert, and an idealist. Currently I'm a Ph.D. student in Neural Science at NYU in Jon Winawer's lab, working on computational models of the human visual system (V1-V3) based on fMRI data from human subjects.

Previously, I did my undergraduate and master's work at MIT in computer science, with a double major in brain and cognitive science. Before that, I was a high schooler with an interest in programming and philosophy of mind, and dreams of being a scientist.
